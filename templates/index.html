About Me

I am a proud member of the Big Pine Paiute Tribe of the Owens Valley. Besides computer science, I enjoy board games, hiking, and language. I'm especially interested in my tribe's native language, Owens Valley Paiute, which is critically endangered and which I am one of many in my community working to learn and revitalize.
